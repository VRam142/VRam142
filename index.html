The Ramani Lab at UC San Francisco develops high-throughput molecular techniques to study gene regulation. We are located at the Gladstone Institute for Data Science & Biotechnology, at 1700 Owens St., in the heart of Mission Bay.

Our lab welcomes individuals of any race, religion, gender identity, caregiver status, political affiliation, or sexual orientation. Black Lives Matter. We are committed to making science equitable and actively being anti-racist. UCSF graduate students and prospective postdocs interested in rotating / applying should contact me to talk about potential projects.
